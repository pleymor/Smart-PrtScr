# Privacy Policy for smart-prtscr

**Last updated: December 9, 2024**

## Overview

smart-prtscr is a screenshot capture tool for Windows. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

smart-prtscr:
- Does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- Does NOT track your usage or behavior
- Does NOT connect to the internet
- Does NOT use analytics or telemetry

## Data Storage

All screenshots are saved locally on your computer in the folder you choose. No data is sent to external servers.

## Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:
- **Screen capture**: To take screenshots of your screen
- **File system access**: To save screenshots to your chosen folder

## Third-Party Services

smart-prtscr does not use any third-party services or APIs.
